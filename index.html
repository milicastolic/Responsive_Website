About Me

My name is Milica Stolić, known as Milla. I’m an tempera painter and pencil drawner/artist currently living in Smederevo (Serbia). I'm an twentyfour year old person. I've always love to draw. Before seven years ago I started to draw more 'seriosly'. I spent a majority of my free time to drawing portraits with colored pencils. I practiced a lot, and become very better at art. I began drawing and filming portraits of people that a broad audience would be familiar with, including celebrities and public figures.

You can watch the videos of my drawing on my youtube channel, and view the pictures on other social media. Links are at the very bottom of this page.
